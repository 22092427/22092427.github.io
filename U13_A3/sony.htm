About Sony


Sony is another standout technology giant well known for its Sony Bravia Televison series and of course the Playstation consoles, they also have a huge selection of other gadgets and appliences. Sony have also been making Mobile phones for an age now.


Sony Xperia Smartphones are packed full of amazing technology such as high quality cameras, displays and intuative UI. Sony's range of Xperia smartphones is quite broad, offering introductory, intermediate and of course flagship models, this means that their smartphones are accessible to a wide range of audiences around the globe. Sony Also run on the Android operating system, meaning that they will work with a wide range of products from a variety of manufacturers.


The wide range of smartphones available from Sony start at a price point of £299 and reach a pricey £1399 for the flagship models. So if being different from the crowd, high powered cameras and versatility floats your boat then a Sony Xperia smartphone may just be the choice for you.
