About Apple


Apple are no doubt a very popular brand that make excellent technology products, though it is worth noteing that when buying an Apple product you are buying into the Apple-verse. This means that Apple products basically only really work with other products from the Apple-verse. You have been warned!


What we have mentioned above shouldn't put you off though, as Apple make fantastic smartphones. Apple's innovation in the design department has left a lot to be desired in recent years as you will see from the four models pictured. But rest assured Apple work hard in the background to keep their smartphones up to date with the latest hardware and software.


Apple smartphones are expensive compared to other brands with prices starting at £429 and reaching a price point of £1199. So if sleek looks, impressive cameras and the Apple-verse appeal to you then Apple may be the smartphone of choice.
