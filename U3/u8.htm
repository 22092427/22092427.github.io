What is Unit 8?

Unit 8 titled Mobile Apps Developement is about understanding the characteristics and uses of mobile apps, before moving on to the designing of a mobile app aimed at children looking to learn the alphabet at ages between 3 and 7 years old.

After the design process it was time to move onto the development of the application, and then once completed and built, move onto the testing phase that ensured the proper working and fucntion of the application developed. Finally after considering feedback and making refinements I reviewed my finished mobile app against the purpose, user requirments and intneded audience.
