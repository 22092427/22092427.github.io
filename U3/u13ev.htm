Unit 13 in detail

Understanding the uses and features of websites

This involved producing a report on two different websites, in two different catagories. Production of this report encouraged us to carry out our own research and to delve deeper into what makes a good website, how websites fulfill their purpose and how they appeal to their target audience through use of features.

Designing a website

For the design process we were given the task of producing a design for an interactive website. In our assignment for the design of the website we were told that the design for a website should be aimed at broad a audience of anyone looking to purchase a mobile phone. Some of the design process can be viewed by clicking the link on this page where you will see a more detailed look at storyboards, structure diagrams, asset logs and test plans.

Develop test and review of the website

For the third assignment it came to developing the website which was done using Notepad. This was a fun process and you can check out the finished website by using the link on this page to open a new tab in which you will be able to move about the website at your leisure. Once I had intially finshed the website I then set about testing it to ensure that the website and all of its functions worked and performed as I intended, following this, some fine tuning was implemented into the website to create what I believe is a well rounded finished product. Finally I set about reviewing my finshed website, this allowed me to be critical of the processs and final product. This also enabled me to come up with ideas and suggestions for improvements that I could make to the website in the future.
