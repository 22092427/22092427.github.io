What is Unit 1?

Unit 1 titled The Online World is all about investigating online services, online communication, issues with operating online and Investigate components of the internet & how digital devices exchange and store information.



During the study of this Unit we heavily relied upon work sheets given out by our tutor and the use of a website called Know It All Ninja to study and sharpen up on knowledge before taking the Unit 1 exam (The Online World).



The Know It All Ninja website can be accessed by clicking on the image of the Know It All "Ninja" on the right hand of the screen.
