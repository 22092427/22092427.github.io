What is Unit 14?

Unit 14 titled Installing and Maintaining Computer Hardware is about understanding hardware components and what they do. In the first assignment for this unit we looked at understanding the benefits and implications of installing and maintaining hardware within technology systems. Delving into this meant we looked at what benefits came with installing hardware in two differnet technology systems, and to what extent the impact could be for individuals or organisations. This was followed up by discussing the strenghts and weaknesses of hardware for a given technology system.

In the second assignment for this Unit we began planning the installation and configuration of hardware components within a technology system. This meant giving thought to compatibility and the cost of components to be installed, as we were given a scenario in which a client had a limited budget. The assignment consisted of research, parts to be intalled and a test plan to be completed after the installation.
